# Alacritty Themes

Three color themes are available. Switch between them by editing the
`import` line in `alacritty.toml`.

## dos-blue.toml
Classic MS-DOS look — blue background (#0000AA), gray text.
Full 16-color CGA palette.

## amber.toml
Monochrome amber terminal, like old text-only business terminals.
Single-hue palette, no ANSI colors — everything renders in amber tones.

## blackout.toml
Simulates old single-color monochrome monitors (white/gray phosphor, no RGB)
All ANSI colors map to white/gray shades, since the real hardware could only display one hue — same idea as amber.toml, but grayscale
